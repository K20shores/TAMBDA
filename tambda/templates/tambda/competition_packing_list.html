Packing List

{% endblock %} {% block competition_content %}
  1. Bring a Printed copy of your partner assignments
  2. Bring the hosting university's rec center release waiver (found under forms).
  3. $10 cash for the workshop and social
  4. $5-$10 cash for your driver
  5. I would bring cash/card for lunch because parent will probably go get it for us. We will probably order on our phones and pay via our phones.
  6. BRING SNACKAGE to eat throughout the competition: granola bars, cheese-its, candy, fruit -- Anything that floats your goat.
  7. Bring a water bottle
  8. Pack:
    • Towel
    • Toiletries (deodorant- I don't want to smell your "dance for 12 hours"- funk)
    • Sleeping bag or blanket
    • Pillow
    • Pjs
    • Bring Texas A&M clothes (shirt, jacket, ...): Rep the Maroon and White at the competition when we aren't dancing
    • Clothes to go home in
    • Comfortable shoes to wear when you aren't dancing
    • Remember to bring a change of clothes to competition to wear afterwards
    • TAMBDA SHIRT!!!
  9. Guys
    • Bring a country western outfit- jeans, country shirt, and cowboy boots​​ (optional)
    • Bring 2 dress shirts (one for Standard/Smooth and one for Latin/Rhythm)
    • Bring a vest (optional)
    • Bring a bow tie (optional)
    • A pair of black dress pants
    • Dance shoes
  10. Girls
    • It's a good idea to put dresses in a dress bag with your name on it somewhere (optional). Bring your dresses hung up on a hanger.
    • Bring a country western outfit (see my previous email for suggestions on what to wear)
    • Standard/Smooth dress
    • Latin/Rhythm Dress
    • Social outfit (optional)
    • Bring an outfit to wear to the social on Friday
    • BRING BOOTY SHORTS or LEOTARD to wear under your dress
    • Bring the appropriate undergarments to wear under your dress
    • Bring comfortable shoes to wear when you aren't dancing
    • Makeup
    • Jacket in case your cold
    • HAIRSPRAY and BOBBY PINS
    • Lipstick
