Footwear

Smooth and Standard

Newcomer, non-costume Attire:

Leads: Dark slacks and white button up collared shirt, optional tie and vest.

Follows: Leotards with wrap skirts, cocktail dresses, long full skirts, jewelry is optional.

Bronze and Silver, costume optional attire:

Same as above but costumes with rhinestones, feathers, and floats, which qualify attire as a costume, are acceptable.

Rhythm and Latin

Newcomer, non-costume attire:

Leads: dark slacks, black or white collared shirt, black T-shirt with no logos or black turtleneck.

Follows: Similar to Standard dress code. Shorter skirts or dresses are acceptable. Similar to purple dress (minus rhinestones).

Bronze and above, costume optional attire:

Similar to white costumes (rhinestones, feathers, and costumes allowed).
